The Idea

Not another app. A quiet introduction to someone who might matter.

The Polyball is more than a night — it’s an atmosphere: a blend of elegance, tradition, and mystery.

Kadray connects those attending the ball who wish to share the evening — not with just anyone, but with someone who feels right.

You answer a few thoughtful questions, tell us a little about yourself, and when the time comes, we introduce you — discreetly and with care.

Maybe it becomes just a good story.
Or maybe, an unforgettable and special memory.

How It Works

Three rounds. Three opportunities. One evening that could change everything.

When the countdown reaches zero, Round 1 begins.

Each round, you’re paired with someone whose answers and energy align with yours. You see just enough to feel a spark — nothing more, nothing less.

If both wish to continue, you’re connected privately. If not, the next round begins.

After three rounds, the experience closes — leaving behind the connections that were meant to happen.

Privacy & Discretion

Some things are meant to stay behind closed doors.

On Kadray, your presence is never public. No profiles to browse, no lists to appear in — only the connection that was meant for you.

Every match happens quietly, known only to the two involved. What begins in confidence stays in confidence.

We believe that true connection doesn’t need an audience — it needs trust, sincerity, and a touch of mystery.
